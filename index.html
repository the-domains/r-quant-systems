Trends The first basic

understood is trends. Trends

bought, or

lower from where he sold

Some may

are “counter-t

rend” traders, but even

need a trend in price

(no

Temperatures

winter approaches. The



demand for
gasoline
gradually trends higher
during the

summer
driving
months. Ground

moisture

trends from moist
to dry
when a drought
approaches,
and interest rates trend
from




high to low or low to high
over time, and so
on. All
these
events, whether
seemingly
natural

unnatural, can create


sustained
price trends in the
futures market, and it is
from
these

trends that we can try
to profit.
Systems like these

are
often referred to as
“trend following

Calling them
“trend following
systems” is correct because
these
systems
cannot
and
do

predict trends.
Instead, they jump on
board

with the trends once they

begun,
and
there are
many empirical studies that

tend to

trend well.

The real difference

trend. A trader

trend as something as

As an example,

Counter-

trend

is

overbought and getting

exits. The real


question is how we can
quantify
these
trading
approaches
and code them
into

profitable trading
systems. Position Sizing

and
Money Management
Along

The

An expert system
